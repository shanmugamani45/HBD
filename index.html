Happy Birthday, My Dear Best Friend ❤️

Some people enter our lives for a season,

but a few become a beautiful chapter in our story.

You are one of those rare souls.

Thank you for the laughter that healed bad days,

the conversations that made ordinary moments special,

and the friendship that never asked for a reason.

Like the moon that quietly brightens the night,

your presence has brought light to many moments of my life.

On this special day, I wish you endless happiness,

countless smiles,

and dreams that find their way to reality.

May your heart stay as kind,

your smile as beautiful,

and your journey as wonderful as the person you are.

No matter where life takes us,

you will always have a special place in my memories.

Happy Birthday, Poetry Queen.

Keep shining, keep smiling, and keep being the amazing person you are. ✨
